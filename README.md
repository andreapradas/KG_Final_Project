# **Building a Knowledge Graph for Chronic Disease Analysis**
## Overview
This project builds a **Knowledge Graph (KG)** from public health survey datasets to model relationships between **patients**, **chronic diseases**, **risk factors** and **clinical measurements**. The KG enables the exploration of hidden patterns, comorbidity pathways and patient risk profiles that are difficult to capture with traditional tabular data.

## Motivation
Chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular disease, and stroke account for **over 74% of global deaths**. Despite the abundance of survey and clinical data, most of it remains fragmented across heterogeneous tabular datasets. This KG approach unifies the data, enabling interpretable and personalized healthcare insights.

# Data Sources
- **Diabetes dataset**
- **Hypertension dataset**
- **Stroke dataset**  

*Note: All datasets are used with proper anonymization and respect for privacy.*
